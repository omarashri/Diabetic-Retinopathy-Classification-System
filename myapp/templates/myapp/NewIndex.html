    Diabetic Retinopathy Dangers

    Diabetic Retinopathy (DR) is the fastest growing cause of blindness, with nearly 415 million diabetic patients at risk worldwide. If caught early, the disease can be treated; if not, it can lead to irreversible blindness.

    About Us

    A Group of Computer Science Bioinformatics Program Ain Shams University Students Developed a diagnostic System to automate DR screening. Referral of eyes with DR to an ophthalmologist for further evaluation and treatment would aid in reducing the rate of vision loss, enabling timely and accurate diagnoses..

    Our Mission

    Is to deliver you a full system that can perfectly detect and classify the DR disease of an image under any condition.

    Our Plan

    Is to develop a digital -clinic alike- system that facilitates th communication between patients and doctors.

    Our Vision

    Is that anyone can use our FREE website to check on himself so reduce the risks of DR disease around the world.
